Random Stuff

Maze Generator

It is an implementation of the Depth First algorithm for the generation of mazes. The basic idea is that we move randomly in unvisited cells removing walls, until all the cells are visited.

Also, it is a very nice screen saver.

Double Pendulum

Simple simulation of a double pendulum. It is made such that the lower mass traces its path on the canvas, in order to show the chaotic nature of the system.

It is also possible to change both masses and the length on the two pendula.
